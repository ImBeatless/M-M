Why Choose Sips?

Engineered and constructed to suit any building, we can create SIPs that completely align to your architectural and design vision – gaining all the mentioned benefits without compromising on appearance to create a space that’s uniquely yours.

The outside environment heavily impacts indoor air quality – SIPs sealed structure restricts airflow to controlled ventilators, only admitting filtered air. They also eliminate voids and thermal bridging, so hazardous and unhygienic mould or rot stay in the past with old building systems.

SIPs are among the most sustainable and eco-friendly building systems available, remaining so for the rest of their lives. Not only do they heavily reduce energy consumption in the home and reduce CO2 levels in the environment, but their materials use less energy in production, and their factory construction method wastes fewer resources than on-site construction.

Manufacturing your unique SIP walls and floors offsite means that installation is rapid and cost-effective. The process enables a streamlined and effortless operation with fewer delays and complications.

Steps on Building Sips

A Guide to the Process

1. Concept collaboration

We discuss your building extension, design plans, and ultimate desires, collaborating on suggestions and recommendations to arrive at the perfect solution.

2. Initial works

We conduct the measurements, drawings, and plans to execute an efficient work programme.

3. SIPs production

Your panels are produced in ideal factory conditions, with automated machinery to ensure materials are securely joined and cut to precision.

4. Seamless transition

Your panels are installed instantly, eliminating a building process that requires you to vacate into offsite accommodation – you can wake up the next day with an upgraded house!
