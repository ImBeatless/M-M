Why did We Choose SIPs?

Why did We Choose SIPs?

SIPs are the cost-effective, sustainable-focused building solution for savvy, modern homeowners. We can create precisely measured panels off-site and install them in a matter of days. Once installed, SIPs provide unrivalled thermal insulation and airtightness, reducing your ongoing energy costs as long as your building lasts. With their extreme strength and quality construction, that’s at least as long as conventional building systems.
